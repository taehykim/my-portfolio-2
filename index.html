About me

Hello World!

My name is Tia. I recently graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in Applied Mathematics. Shortly after graduating, I started working as a Business Intelligence Analyst in San Diego, California. As a BI analyst, I had the opportunity to directly work with the engineering team to build small internal web pages for their Sales Team and this is when I realized that I really enjoy building and creating things on the web.

I am ready for new adventures as a software engineer!

Recently, I finished a 14-week full stack web development program in Irvine, California. A few technologies that I've been using are HTML5, CSS, Sass, Boostrap4, Flexbox, jQuery, React.js, Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, Git, webpack, npm, Babel, and more.

Outside of work ...

I enjoy being in the nature, exploring and trying new coffee, weight lifting, rock climbing and learning new languages and culture!
